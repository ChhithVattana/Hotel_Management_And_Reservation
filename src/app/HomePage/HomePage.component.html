ABOUT US

Sunway Hotel Phnom Penh is a 4-star Deluxe hotel located within the heart of Cambodia’s “green belt” capital Phnom Penh. Designed for travellers seeking international standards whether on business or leisure, the 138-room hotel features a collection of guestrooms and suites, some with private balconies featuring views of the city’s sprawling boulevards. Each guestroom and suite is equipped with comprehensive amenities that include complimentary Wi-Fi and over 60 local and international satellite channels including movies, news, documentaries, sports and music.
